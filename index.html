The Snack that started it all 🍪

Gratia Cookies are handcrafted using a cherished family recipe passed down through generations. Every batch is made fresh with premium flour, real butter, and just the right pinch of nutmeg - the way it's always been done.

Watching you relish in childhood memories that our cookies brings has had us reminiscing about special memories of our own.

Whether you're curled up with a movie, sharing with friends at a gathering, or sneaking a handful at your desk - Gratia hits different. Crunchy, golden-brown, and completely irresistible.

No artificial flavours. No preservatives. Just pure, honest, delicious cookies - the way grandma made it. 🤎
